By contributing to OpenSecDash, you confirm that:

1. You created the contribution yourself, or you have the right to submit it.
2. You allow the OpenSecDash project to use, modify, distribute, and sublicense your contribution as part of the project.
3. Your contribution can be distributed under the project's open-source license.
4. You still keep your own rights to your contribution and may use it elsewhere.

This CLA is intended to be simple and low-friction. In practice, confirmation may happen through a pull-request checkbox, bot comment, or explicit statement such as:

```text
I have read and agree to the OpenSecDash CLA.
```

Thank you for helping make OpenSecDash better for the homelab community.
